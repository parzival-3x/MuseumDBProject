Tours

From the extensive MFAH art collections—with objects spanning the world and human creative history—to the ever-changing schedule of thematic exhibitions, the Museum offers a diverse range of tour topics to suit your interests. Visit as a self-guided group, and you and your guests can explore at your own pace. For a deeper dive into the galleries, request a docent-guided tour.
